About Createwith

Made slowly.
Made to stay.

We make things that earn their place in a room. Small-batch objects (lamps and interior pieces) shaped from walnut and brass, designed with the patience that mass production never allows.

Each piece begins as a sketch, becomes a prototype, and only reaches you when it is ready. No shortcuts. No compromises with materials. Nothing made to be replaced in three years.

Createwith is a Norwegian design brand for people who prefer fewer, better things.

Our philosophy

Varighet

"In an age of disposable things, we are interested in the permanent: objects that ask to be kept, that acquire meaning with use."

Material honesty

Walnut ages to a richer colour. Brass develops a patina. We choose materials that improve over decades, never degrade.

Small batches

We make a limited number of each piece. This is not manufactured scarcity. It is the only way to maintain the standard we require of ourselves.

Designed to repair

Every lamp is assembled with access in mind. If something needs attention in ten years, we want to be able to give it.

Ready to begin

Something worth waiting for.
