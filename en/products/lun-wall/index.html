Lun Wall

Status

In development

From

NOK 3,100

Materials

Walnut Brass

The wall-mounted sibling to the pendant. The same turned walnut form, a brass wall plate, and an adjustable arm that lets you direct the light where it is needed. Works beautifully beside a bed, above a shelf, or in a hallway.

The wall plate is solid brass and is fixed with concealed fittings. The arm is jointed so the cone of light can be adjusted. The cable can be routed along the wall surface or concealed within the wall on request.
