Materials

Walnut Brass

A small sculptural object for the working surface. Solid walnut with a precision brass inlay. It does not hold pens or papers — it holds a moment of quiet in a busy desk. The kind of thing that improves every room it enters.

The object is turned and hand-polished from a single piece of walnut. The brass inlay is precision-milled and pressed in without adhesive. The base is lined with untreated suede leather to protect the working surface.
